Character Details

Detailed profiles of major Naruto Shippuden characters featured in the gallery.

Naruto Uzumaki

Naruto is a shinobi of the Hidden Leaf Village who begins life as an outcast but grows into one of the most influential ninja in history through perseverance and empathy.

Village: Hidden Leaf (Konohagakure)

Role: Protagonist • Shinobi

Abilities
  • Shadow Clone Technique
  • Rasengan
  • High stamina and endurance
  • Advanced combat instincts
Personality
  • Determined and optimistic
  • Protective of friends
  • Emotionally resilient

Sasuke Uchiha

Sasuke is a prodigy of the Uchiha clan whose life is shaped by tragedy, driving him toward revenge and eventually forcing him to question justice and loyalty.

Village: Hidden Leaf (formerly)

Role: Rival • Shinobi

Abilities
  • Sharingan & advanced visual jutsu
  • Chidori (Lightning Style)
  • Swordsmanship
  • Tactical combat intelligence
Personality
  • Calm and reserved
  • Driven by vengeance
  • Highly analytical

Sakura Haruno

Sakura is a highly skilled medical ninja whose intelligence and chakra control allow her to provide both frontline combat power and life-saving medical support.

Village: Hidden Leaf

Role: Medical Ninja • Shinobi

Abilities
  • Advanced medical ninjutsu
  • Exceptional chakra control
  • Superhuman strength
  • Battlefield healing
Personality
  • Intelligent and disciplined
  • Emotionally strong
  • Protective and loyal

Kakashi Hatake

Kakashi is a seasoned jonin and mentor of Team 7, renowned for copying enemy techniques and teaching the importance of teamwork and survival.

Village: Hidden Leaf

Role: Sensei • Jonin

Abilities
  • Sharingan (Copy Ninja)
  • Lightning Blade (Chidori)
  • Advanced tactical combat
  • Stealth and intelligence gathering
Personality
  • Calm and composed
  • Strategic thinker
  • Protective mentor

Jiraiya

Jiraiya is one of the Legendary Sannin, a powerful ninja and writer who played a critical role in training future leaders while gathering intelligence across nations.

Village: Hidden Leaf

Role: Mentor • Legendary Sannin

Abilities
  • Toad summoning techniques
  • Sage Mode
  • Rasengan mastery
  • Espionage and reconnaissance
Personality
  • Wise but eccentric
  • Protective mentor
  • Strong moral compass
